Digital Security

Introduction to Digital Security

SystemTrust is a leading Information Systems Security (ISS) service provider in Nigeria. Working in close partnership with strategic global partners, SystemTrust offer cutting-edge and cost-effective IS security solutions to a broad range of customers. Our expertise range from IS Security Consulting, Authentication, Encryption, Anti-Virus, Intrusion/Extrusion Prevention, Centralized Network Backup, Cyber Security, and Digital Forensics.

In 2010, SystemTrust (Digital Security Division) constituted the FirstForensicsTM team, which is Nigeria’s leading provider of digital forensics services. SystemTrust is the partner of AccessData Corporation in Nigeria. AccessData Corporation is the world’s largest digital forensics software company.

FirstForensicsTM draws from the extensive pool of experts at her disposal both in Nigeria and abroad. We have the capacity to implement large-scale digital forensics examinations and data recovery within a short period of time, as may be required by multi-national corporations, government, military or law enforcement agencies. We provide comprehensive digital security to prevent breaches while offering world-class forensics support when breaches occur.

Our digital security services include:

  • Email/Web/Application/Data/Network Security
  • Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity
  • Centralized Network Backup
  • Digital Forensics Laboratory Design and Implementation
  • CCTV
  • Information Systems Security Consulting
  • High-impact Training
