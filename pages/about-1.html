About Us

Welcome to our website. SystemTrust is an exciting, fast-growing company with a friendly culture and strong dedication to customer satisfaction.


SystemTrust [ICT] Limited was established in 2001 with the objective of providing world-class information communication technology (ICT), power and digital security solutions to organizations and businesses.
Since inception, SystemTrust has grown through forming strategic alliances as well as enhancing her in-house capabilities in order to meet the ever-changing needs of the customers.

The company has been noted for the provision of prompt and efficient services. This has been as a result of our philosophy of providing client-tailored, service-based innovative solutions and in-depth understanding of our customersâ€™ individual needs backed by highly experienced professionals.

SystemTrust has focused on two core areas namely: POWER SOLUTIONS, and DIGITAL SECURITY. These core areas have transformed into independent business Divisions.


Our service delivery is anchored on our core values of integrity, quality, excellence and customer care.


Our customers, employees, technical partners and investors are the nucleus of our success. SystemTrust is committed to all these stakeholders. We remain committed to our environment and shall continue to discharge our corporate social responsibility innovatively.
